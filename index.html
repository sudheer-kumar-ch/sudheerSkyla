Our Story

The city of Hyderabad has been a thriving global destination for businesses, medical tourists, and tourism at large. However, travel also evolved from royal expeditions to purpose-driven journeys; and we wanted to fill these journeys with warmth and comfort.

Starting in 2010 with one serviced apartment, Skyla today operates across 10 properties in the city, and has completed over 1 million room nights so far. The mark of our achievement continues to be our Home Away From Home experiences offered to guests in the city for business, leisure, or medical purposes.
